## ESC Guideline Recommendations (2019 Dyslipidaemia)

### ESC Published Recommendations:

**For Very High-Risk Patients:**
> "In very high-risk patients with ASCVD whose LDL-C levels are not at goal despite treatment with a maximally tolerated statin dose in combination with ezetimibe, combination therapy with a PCSK9 inhibitor is recommended"
> **Class I, Level A**

**For Recurrent Events:**
> "For patients with ASCVD who experience a second vascular event within 2 years while taking maximally tolerated statin therapy, a PCSK9 inhibitor may be considered"
> **Class IIb, Level C** (at time of publication; upgraded in 2024)

### LEC Pipeline Derived:
> "PCSK9 inhibitors (evolocumab, alirocumab) should be considered in adults with ASCVD on maximally tolerated statin therapy with LDL-C above target"
> **Class IIa, Level A**

### Concordance Analysis:

| Parameter | ESC 2019 | LEC Pipeline | Match |
|-----------|----------|--------------|-------|
| Effect Estimate | HR 0.85 | HR 0.84 | YES |
| GRADE Certainty | High | High | YES |
| Evidence Level | A | A | YES |
| Recommendation Class | I (very high risk) | IIa | PARTIAL |

**Note on Class Discrepancy:**
- ESC Class I applies specifically to "very high risk with LDL-C not at goal"
- LEC pipeline uses broader population definition
- ESC has nuanced recommendations by risk stratum
- Clinical interpretation: Both support strong recommendation for appropriate patients
